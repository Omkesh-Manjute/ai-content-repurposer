# Project Report: AI Content Repurposer

## 1. Project Overview
Project Name: AI Content Repurposer  
Status: Active Development  

Vision:
To automatically turn long YouTube videos into short, easy-to-read notes, social media captions, and reel scripts using Artificial Intelligence. This tool saves a lot of time and makes content creation much easier.

## 2. Who is this for?
This app is built for a variety of users:
- Students & Learners: For quick revisions and making study notes from long tutorials.
- Content Creators: To easily get short clip scripts and social media captions without watching the whole video.
- Educators: To summarize their lectures for students.
- Professionals: To quickly get the main points from long meetings or webinars.

## 3. The Problem & Our Solution
The Problem:
There is too much long video content online, and nobody has the time to watch it all. Watching a 1-hour video just to find a few pieces of information is frustrating. Also, creating notes or thinking of ideas for shorts/reels takes a lot of manual effort.

Our Solution:
A simple web app where you just paste a YouTube link. The system does the heavy lifting for you by:
1. Extracting the video transcript.
2. Understanding the text using AI.
3. Automatically generating three useful things:
   - Notes: Clear, bulleted key takeaways.
   - Captions: Ready-to-post written captions with a good tone.
   - Reel Scripts: Engaging 30-to-60-second scripts with a hook, main body, and a call-to-action.

## 4. Technology Stack
We used modern technologies to make the app fast, look great, and process AI smoothly.

Frontend:
- Framework: Next.js 16 (React 19)
- Styling: Tailwind CSS 
- Animations: Framer Motion for a smooth, premium feel
- Icons: Lucide React
- Theme: Dark and Light mode support

Backend & API:
- Backend Technologies: Node.js (Next.js API Routes) and Python.
- Python Engine: We use a Python-based engine (using yt-dlp and moviepy) to download, process, and cut videos.
- Video Transcript: youtube-transcript algorithm
- YouTube API: googleapis

Artificial Intelligence:
- AI Provider: Google Gemini API is used to read the video text and summarize it smartly.

## 5. How It Works
1. Input: You open the website and paste a YouTube link.
2. Action: You click the Generate button.
3. Processing: The app checks the link, gets the text from the video, and sends it to the AI to summarize.
4. Output: The screen nicely shows your Notes, Captions, and Scripts in separate tabs.

Key Features:
- Fast: Gets you results quickly using top-tier AI.
- Beautiful UI: Looks modern and feels very smooth to use.
- Easy Export: You can copy your generated text with one click.

## 6. How We Measure Success
We will know the project is successful when:
- People save hours of time they would have spent watching long videos.
- The AI gives highly accurate and useful answers.
- Users keep coming back to use the app again.

## 7. Future Plans
- Language Support: Automatically translating results into Hindi, Spanish, etc.
- Automatic Video Clipping: Fully automating the cutting and downloading of the most viral parts of the video.
- Tone Selection: Letting users choose if they want the output to sound formal, casual, or viral.
- User Accounts: A login system so users can save their past work.
