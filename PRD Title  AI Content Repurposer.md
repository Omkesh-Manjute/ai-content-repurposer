Title:  AI Content Repurposer

Subtitle:  Convert Long Videos into Notes, Captions & Reels
Introduction :
Today, a lot of video content is available online But watching long videos is time-consumingStudents and creators need quick and useful information This project uses AI to convert videos into notes, captions, and short scripts It makes learning and content creation faster and easier

Problem Statement
There is too much content available, but less time to consume it.
Watching long videos is not always practical.
Students and creators need short and useful content.
Creating notes or reels manually takes a lot of time

Solution
An AI-based tool that converts long videos into short content.
User just pastes a YouTube link. 
System automatically:
Generates notes
Creates captions
Generates reel/script content

Working 
Take video link as input
Extract transcript
Analyze important points
Generate notes, captions, and scripts
